It's a blog!

Date Fri 10 January 2020 Tags general

Well, well. 30 Years in IT and i finally came around adding a personal site along with a "blog". Newfangled technology. But everyone kept bugging me about it, so here you go. I sure hope it makes sense to someone.

Content?

As everyone knows, a blog needs content. So what content can you expect here?

  • Introductory and tutorial stuff for the Isomer and Hackerfleet projects
  • News about my projects
  • Rants against clown computing and for decentralization
  • Sometimes i produce creative content like music or even games

So, i hope you'll enjoy consuming this creative mess as much as i enjoy making it. If you like it too much, you might even consider donating.
